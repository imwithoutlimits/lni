Plain English summary: We collect your name, email, country and city when you fill in a form on this website. We use that information only to connect you with an LNI network and to send you relevant communications. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. You can ask us to delete it at any time.

01

Who We Are

Lehem Network International (LNI) is a global ministry network headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria. This privacy policy applies to the website located at lni.netlify.app and any associated digital services operated by LNI Global.

For the purposes of data protection law, LNI Global is the data controller responsible for your personal information.

02

What Data We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us through the forms on this website. This may include:

  • First and last name — so we can address you personally
  • Email address — so we can respond to you and connect you with your local network
  • Country and city — so we can match you with the correct LNI network in your area
  • Phone number — only if you choose to provide it on the Pioneer Interest form
  • Testimony text — if you submit a testimony, which may be displayed publicly on the website
  • Prayer requests — submitted through the prayer request form, stored securely
  • Ministry background — only on the Pioneer form, and only if you choose to share it

We do not collect payment information, government identification, or any sensitive personal data.

03

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used only for the following purposes:

  • To connect you with your nearest LNI network and its leader
  • To follow up on your registration, pioneer interest, or enquiry
  • To display approved testimonies on the global live feed (only testimony text — your name is optional and displayed only if you provide it)
  • To send you relevant communications about LNI networks, events, and teachings — only if you have engaged with us through a form
  • To notify our team of new form submissions so we can respond promptly

We will never use your data to send unrelated marketing, spam, or third-party promotions.

04

Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a secure cloud database service operated by Google LLC. Firebase is compliant with GDPR, ISO 27001, and SOC 1/2/3 standards. Your data may be stored on servers located in Europe or the United States depending on Firebase's infrastructure.

Form notification emails are processed through Web3Forms, a secure email relay service. These emails are not stored permanently by Web3Forms — they are forwarded directly to our team's inbox.

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, including database security rules that restrict read/write access.

05

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Your data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your local LNI network leader — when you register to join a specific network, the network leader may receive your name and email so they can welcome you
  • With Google (Firebase) — as the infrastructure provider for our database, as described above
  • If required by law — if we are legally compelled to disclose information by a court or regulatory authority
06

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data. To exercise any of them, contact us at global@lehemnetwork.org:

  • Right to access — you can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to deletion — you can ask us to delete your data at any time and we will do so within 30 days
  • Right to object — you can object to us processing your data for any reason
  • Right to withdraw consent — if you have given consent to any processing, you can withdraw it at any time

If you are located in the European Union, you also have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you are located in California, USA, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

07

Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies. Specifically:

  • Firebase Analytics — we use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (page views, general location, device type). This data is anonymous and aggregated. No individual is identified.
  • We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party marketing cookies.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use this website.
